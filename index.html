Scientific Researcher and Pre-Doctoral Student. Strong background in research, academic outreach and active teaching. Collaborator in the FWF / Austrian Science Fund- sponsored research project “Entanglements between Cuba and the GDR: Mobilities, Exchanges, Circulations within the Council for Mutual Economic Assistance” at the Institute of Economic and Social History of the University of Vienna (https://socialist-entanglements.univie.ac.at/). My research focuses on the history of development policies and temporary migration programs in the “Socialist World System”. My publications have focused on Cuban foreign policy and on Cuban Iinternationalism.
